# Overview

This repository contains writing samples prepared to demonstrate my ability to synthesize scientific literature, analyze data-driven concepts, and clearly communicate research findings.

## Samples Description

### "Hygiene and Body Site Affect Your Skin Microbiome"
-  skin_microbiome_analysis.pdf: This writing sample was originally developed for a Public Health Genomics course and focuses on the relationship between hygiene practices, body site variation, and skin microbiome composition.

### "Mobile DNA Activity in Parkinson’s Disease: A Locus-Specific View of Endogenous Retroviruses"
- researchday_abstract_EBA.pdf: This writing sample was developed for GWSPH Research Day and focuses on dysregulated HERV loci in PD and transcriptional pathogenesis.
- bioArxiv preprint: https://doi.org/10.64898/2026.07.03.736370
